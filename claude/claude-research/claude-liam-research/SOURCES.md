## RESEARCH-PLUGIN FACT-CHECK NOTE (the load-bearing correction for this reel)
Per the book-level FACTCHECK.md §2: **a generic "Research" plugin is NOT in the current official
Anthropic catalog.** The official research plugin is **`bio-research`**, scoped to life sciences
(single-cell RNA analysis, bioinformatics) — it does not do the *market* research this chapter
describes. The market-research workflows the chapter teaches (source synthesis, competitor
intelligence, literature/market review, gap analysis, citation tracking, decision support) **are
real Cowork capabilities**, but they are not a dedicated, named "Research plugin" product.

**How this reel handles it:**
- Built around the **capability/workflows**, never a named SKU. Narration says "research support,"
  "pointed at your market," "the same engine" — it never claims an official "Research plugin" exists.
- The chapter's install instruction ("Install the research plugin from the plugin sidebar") is
  **dropped** from the narration.
- The reel's title/subtitle cards ("Claude, Scouting" / "The Research Plugin") carry the book
  chapter's framing as reel branding only — not a spoken product claim.

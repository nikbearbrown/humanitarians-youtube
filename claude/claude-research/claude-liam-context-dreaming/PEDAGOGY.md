## 2. Prediction-before-reveal moments

- B00 answers the cold-open ask in compressed form; the body then earns each
  line of that answer — the viewer holds a map and watches it fill in.
- B06 "Hold that thought" plants the CLAUDE.md failure mode one beat before
  B07 shows it.
- B18 "notice, as they land, which discipline they are borrowed from" invites
  the viewer to guess before B20 names optimistic locking and B21 confirms it
  from her own Q&A — the reveal is HER admission, not our assertion.
- B25 "build all of that, and you are done? No." — explicit prediction break
  before the ceiling.
- B40 sets up the viewer's own objection ("dreaming looks expensive") and
  inverts it with her reported accounting.

## 7. Honesty pedagogy (this episode's own subject demands it)

- Results claims teach the viewer to distinguish mechanism (verifiable in the
  transcript) from reported outcome (not independently verified): B24 and B40
  stamp "speaker-reported" ON SCREEN — the epistemics are part of the lesson.
- The YOUR TURN prompt operationalizes the episode: the viewer runs a manual
  dreaming pass tonight, and the discussion lines teach evaluation criteria
  (evidence quotes present? prevalence counted? any deletions proposed?) —
  transfer, not recall.
- Tier 3 speaker handled per brief: credited by name in B00 narration and on
  the B41 card; zero imagery of her.
